Step-by-Step User Manual

A complete guide on how to navigate, book, and manage hostels on the platform.

Made with ❤️ by Kazi Chris

1. Getting Started (Registration & Login)

Before using the platform, users must create an account to securely save data and track bookings.

1 Creating an Account

  • Open the BkonnectHomes website in your browser.
  • Click the Log In button at the top right of the navigation bar.
  • Click "Sign up free" at the bottom of the login form.
  • Select your role by clicking either the 🎓 Student or 🏠 Owner button.
  • Fill out your full name, email, phone number, and password. If you are a student, select your University from the dropdown menu.
  • Click Create Account. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard.

2 Logging In

  • Navigate to the Log In page.
  • Enter your registered email and password.
  • Click Log In to access your secure dashboard.

2. Guide for Students Student

As a student, BkonnectHomes allows you to easily find, compare, and book the perfect room.

1 Finding a Hostel

  • Go to the Home Page.
  • Use the Search Bar at the top to type in a specific location (e.g., "Gayaza") or hostel name.
  • Use the circular Category Filters (e.g., "Bugema Uni", "Private Hostels") to instantly filter properties that match your requirements.

2 Comparing Properties

Not sure which hostel to pick? You can compare up to 3 properties side-by-side to make the best decision!
  • While browsing the Home Page, look for the small Scale Icon in the top-right corner of any hostel card.
  • Click the Scale Icon to add that hostel to your Compare List.
  • A floating button will appear at the bottom of your screen showing how many properties you have selected (e.g., "Compare (2)").
  • Click the floating button to open the Compare Engine. Here, you will see a detailed grid comparing prices, proximity, and shared amenities.

3 Placing a Booking

  • Click on any hostel card to view its full details.
  • Scroll down to the Available Rooms section.
  • Find the room type you want (e.g., Single Room or Double Room) and click the Book Now button.
  • A success message will appear confirming your reservation.

4 Managing Your Bookings

  • Click Dashboard in the top navigation bar.
  • In your Student Dashboard, you will see the My Bookings section.
  • This ledger shows exactly which hostels you booked, the exact date and time the booking was placed, and the current status of your reservation (Pending/Confirmed).

3. Guide for Hostel Owners Owner

If you own a property, you can list it on the platform to reach thousands of students instantly.

1 Adding a New Hostel

  • Log into your Owner account and click Dashboard.
  • Click the Add New Hostel button.
  • Fill out the property details: Name, Category (University vs. Private), Description, Street Address, Area, and the closest University.
  • Click Save Hostel. Your hostel is now created but currently has no rooms!

2 Adding Rooms & Pricing

Students cannot book your hostel until you add at least one Room Type with a price!
  • In your dashboard, click on the hostel you just created.
  • Scroll down to the Add Room section.
  • Select the Room Type (Single, Double, Suite, Custom).
  • Enter the Monthly Rent price (e.g., 500000).
  • Type out the amenities provided (e.g., "Wi-Fi, Personal Desk, Shared Bathroom").
  • Upload a high-quality photo of the room and click Add Room.
  • Your hostel is now live and visible to all students on the Home Page!

4. Guide for Administrators Admin

Admins oversee the platform, ensuring bookings flow smoothly and monitoring total growth.

1 Accessing the Master Ledger

  • Log in using the secure Admin credentials.
  • Click Admin Panel in the top navigation menu.
  • At the top of the dashboard, you will see live statistics: Total Users, Total Hostels, and Total Bookings.

2 Monitoring Activity

  • Scroll down to the Master Bookings Ledger.
  • This table displays every single booking made on the entire platform.
  • You can view the exact Date and Time the booking was made, the Student's name, the Hostel name, the specific Room ID, and the current payment/booking Status.
  • Use this ledger to contact owners and students to finalize their offline payments and rental agreements.
